Hi, I'm Josh! I'm a software engineer and aspiring technical game designer with more than 7 years of C# experience. My goal is to create truly innovative experiences with the player's enjoyment at their heart and soul.

About Me

To me, innovation is something much more than the empty word so often thrown around today for the current trendy technology or the latest scheme to claw more money from players' wallets. Innovation means trying a new idea or a unique twist on an existing concept, with the intent to provide a truly better experience that pushes the medium forward in a meaningful way. I want to be a part of the process by which those innovative ideas are iterated and refined into something that changes the conversations around how we work and how we play for the better.
